Disc Finder is the ultimate disc golf tool to search / compare discs across disc manufacturers and build your bag. All features are FREE and there is no account login.

Bag Builder Features:
• Create and customize up to five bags by name
• Rapidly add discs by searching and adding to bag
• Add custom discs with unique flight details
• Add disc color, plastic, weight, and notes to all discs
• View gaps in your bag using the Gap Report
• Organize and sort discs in up five bags

Disc Finder Search Features:
• Find similar discs based on your search criteria
• Bag Builder feature to save discs to your Bag/Inventory
• Compare multiple disc manufacturers simultaneously
• Search for a by specific mold, brand or Flight Number
• Compare similar plastic types across manufacturers
• Purchase discs through our retail sponsor 1010 Discs
