Disc Finder is the ultimate disc golf companion tool for searching and comparing discs across various manufacturers, helping you build the perfect bag. The app works offline, requiring no account login or internet connection for bag building, capabilities not offered by web-based alternatives. Disc Finder also features a wider selection of disc molds than any online web app, making it easier to compare options and find replacement discs. Future updates will introduce new features, additional manufacturers, and the latest disc releases.



Bag Builder Features:
• Create and customize up to five named bags
• Rapidly add discs by searching and adding to bag
• Add custom discs with unique flight details
• Add disc color, plastic, weight, and notes to all discs
• Add a custom photo of each disc
• Visualize inventory gaps and get disc suggestions
• Organize and sort discs into custom bags

Disc Finder Search Features:
• Find similar discs based on your search criteria
• Bag Builder feature to save discs to your Bag/Inventory
• Compare multiple disc manufacturers simultaneously
• Search for a specific mold, brand or Flight Number
• Compare similar plastic types across manufacturers
• Purchase discs through our retail sponsor 1010 Discs
