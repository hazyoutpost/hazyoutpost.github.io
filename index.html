Ultimate Disc Golf Companion

Search and compare discs across various manufacturers, helping you build the perfect bag. Works offline with no account required - capabilities not offered by other alternatives.

Why Choose Disc Finder?

  • True native iOS/Android app
  • Works completely offline
  • Extensive, up-to-date database
  • Your data stays private
  • All base features are free
  • Not a web-based platform
  • Not AI-generated shovelware
  • Not a subscription-based model

Features

Bag Builder Features

  • Create and customize up to five named bags
  • Rapidly add discs by searching and adding to bag
  • Add custom discs with unique flight details
  • Add disc color, plastic, weight, and notes to all discs
  • Add a custom photo of each disc
  • Visualize inventory gaps and get disc suggestions
  • Organize and sort discs into custom bags

Disc Finder Search Features

  • Find similar discs based on your search criteria
  • Bag Builder feature to save discs to your Bag/Inventory
  • Compare multiple disc manufacturers simultaneously
  • Search for a specific mold, brand or Flight Number
  • Compare similar plastic types across manufacturers
  • Purchase discs through our retail sponsor 1010 Discs

Disc Finder Pro

  • Add unlimited discs and bags to your inventory
  • Export and import data for use across all devices
  • Sync data with smart merge or do a full restore
  • Add and Delete bags for greater organization
  • Future Pro features will be included for a lifetime
